Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I place an order?

    To place an order, simply visit our Shop page and select the items you want to purchase. Then proceed to checkout and follow the instructions to complete your order.

  • Do you offer repair services?

    Yes, we offer repair services for guitars. You can visit our Repair page for more information on the types of repairs we offer and how to submit a repair request.

  • Do you sell merchandise?

    Yes, we have a selection of merchandise available for purchase. Check out our Merch page to see our current offerings.

  • Can I join discussions on your website?

    Yes, we have a Discussions page where you can engage with other guitar enthusiasts, ask questions, and share your experiences.

  • Do you offer guitar lessons?

    Yes, we offer guitar lessons for players of all levels. Visit our Lessons page to learn more and sign up for lessons.
