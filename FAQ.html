Frequently Asked Questions

Shop FAQ

Q: How can I track my order?

A: You can track your order by logging into your account and checking the order status.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries.

Q: Can I return an item if I'm not satisfied?

A: Yes, we have a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of purchase.

Repair FAQ

Q: How long does a typical guitar repair take?

A: It depends on the extent of the repair, but most repairs are completed within 1-2 weeks.

Q: Do you offer pickup and delivery for repairs?

A: Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services within a certain radius for an additional fee.

Q: What types of repairs do you specialize in?

A: We specialize in guitar setups, electronics repair, fretwork, and more.

Q: Can I book a repair appointment online?

A: Yes, you can book a repair appointment through our website or by giving us a call.

Lessons FAQ

Q: What is the duration of each guitar lesson?

A: Our standard guitar lessons are typically 30 minutes long, but we also offer 1-hour sessions.

Q: Do you provide instruments for lessons?

A: Yes, we provide instruments for lessons, but students are encouraged to bring their own if possible.

Q: Are your instructors qualified?

A: Yes, all our instructors are highly qualified and experienced in teaching guitar.

Q: Can I schedule lessons for evenings or weekends?

A: Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate our students' needs.

Discussion FAQ

Q: How can I participate in your online guitar discussions?

A: You can join our online forums by creating an account on our website and engaging with other members.

Q: Are there any rules for participating in discussions?

A: Yes, we have community guidelines in place to ensure respectful and constructive discussions.

Q: Can I ask for guitar advice from other members?

A: Absolutely! Our community is here to help each other with guitar-related questions and advice.

Q: Do you organize any in-person jam sessions or meetups?

A: Yes, we occasionally organize jam sessions and meetups for our members to connect in person.

Merch FAQ

Q: What types of merchandise do you offer?

A: We offer a variety of merchandise including t-shirts, hats, posters, and guitar accessories.

Q: Do you ship merchandise internationally?

Q: Can I customize merchandise with my own designs?
